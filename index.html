Artifact 3: Clustering Analysis with K-Means

Overview

This artifact demonstrates the use of K-Means clustering to segment customer data and reveal hidden patterns. It outlines the motivation behind the choice of K-Means, its applications in business analytics, and its role in customer segmentation.
